About Dr App Association

Dr App Association is a digital platform designed for business and investment associations to support more efficient operations and sustainable growth. The app connects members, optimises management processes, and enhances collaboration in a secure, unified environment.

Main Features:
  • Member Management: Easy registration, classification, and management of member information.
  • Networking & Collaboration: Strengthen business relationships and expand investment opportunities.
  • News & Notifications: Timely updates, policies, and internal information sharing.
  • Meetings & Events: Create, schedule, and manage association meetings, conferences, and events.
  • Document Library: Provide important documents, reports, and resources for all members.
  • Secure Access: Ensure data safety for association leaders and members.

Dr App Association helps associations increase engagement, improve transparency, and support sustainable development in the business and investment community.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Phone number (for authentication and customer support only), Name (used for identification, not for tracking or advertising).
  • Non-Personal Data: Device information (model, OS version), app usage statistics, error logs, and crash data.
  • Camera Access: Used only for QR code scanning. Images and videos are never stored, uploaded, or shared.
  • Firebase Services: Used for push notifications, analytics, and crash reporting. Firebase may collect device identifiers and diagnostics, but does not collect phone numbers or names unless explicitly provided by the user.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • App functionality: Authentication, QR scanning, real-time features, error diagnosis.
  • Analytics: Understanding user interactions and improving the app.
  • Security: Preventing fraud, enhancing safety, maintaining system integrity.
  • Notifications: Sending important service updates via Firebase Cloud Messaging.
  • No personal data is used for third-party advertising or tracking.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • Data may be shared only with service providers (e.g., Firebase) for analytics, notifications, and crash handling, or legal authorities when required by law.
  • All third-party service providers are required to comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Users may request data deletion at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Data is deleted promptly upon request.

5. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent (if applicable)
  • Contact us for any privacy-related concerns

6. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We comply with applicable data protection laws.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted immediately. Parents or guardians may contact us to request deletion.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on our website, and take effect immediately upon posting. We encourage users to review this policy regularly.
